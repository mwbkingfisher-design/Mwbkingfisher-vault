
“What did you say? Nails? That’s sick; I can't imagine how painful that must have been,” said Joe.

The podcast about two men talking about unsolved crimes was the most listened-to production. Everyone wanted to be hypnotized and scared by the drastic, uncensored scenes.

“The victim was tied to the tree with an almost invisible wire. The killer, with precise hits of a hammer, attached the victim to the tree with huge nails.”

Nigel talked about this with huge enthusiasm. Almost as if he approved that way of torture. 

Joe finished the episode as usual. 

“That, like so many other cases, has still not been solved to this day. Thank you for listening, and be careful. You never know who may be the person standing next to you.”

“From the murder scene to the music scene. Let's go!" Nigel said, clicking the ‘live’ button.

Or at least... he thought he clicked it.

"Oh, come on, nobody is listening. We are off.”

Something struck Joe.

“Nigel, when we are off… this ‘live’ button should blink or not?”
